Xiaodong Wu

Xiaodong Wu (吴晓东) is currently a PhD. student in Electronic and Computer Engineering Apartment at Queen's Univercity at Kingston, supervised by Professor Jianbing Ni. I work on the AI security including adversarial attacks, malware detection, trustworthy AI, etc. Before that, I did my masters at KAUST and bachelors at UESTC.
